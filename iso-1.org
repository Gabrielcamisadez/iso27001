* Accreditation vs Certification

Certification and accreditation are distinct terms in the ISO ecosystem

*Accreditation* ->
Validates that a certification body is competent to perform certification audits

*Certification* ->
Validates that an organization meets the requirements of a specific ISO standard


Accreditation evaluates a CB's competency to initially and continually conduct certification audits
