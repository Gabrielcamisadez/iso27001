#+title: Audit Team Roles
#+author: gabriel

* intro
Clearyl defined roles and responsibilities are essential

Audit team composition depends on
- auditee size and complexity
- scope of the audit

Collective competency in necessary to evaluate ISMS and security controls

*Key takeaway* --> understanding team roles aids in conducting audits appropriately, whether you are new or transitioning to ISO audits

* lead auditor
*Role* --> leads the audit process, both client-facing and team-facing
- plans the audit (time,scope,sampling,team,schedule,budget)
- conducts opening and interviews with auditee stakeholders
- reviews evidence, determines nonconformities, and finalizes the audit report



** importance of audit team composition
- *Consistency* --> defined roles ensure audits achieve objectives across clients
- *Efficiency* --> clear roles enable effective collaboration and work distribution
- *Depth* --> a well-composed team with the right number of auditors and technical expertise ensures thorough audits
