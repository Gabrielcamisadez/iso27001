#+title: Audit Team Roles
#+author: gabriel

* intro
Clearyl defined roles and responsibilities are essential

Audit team composition depends on
- auditee size and complexity
- scope of the audit

Collective competency in necessary to evaluate ISMS and security controls

*Key takeaway* --> understanding team roles aids in conducting audits appropriately, whether you are new or transitioning to ISO audits

* lead auditor
*Role* --> leads the audit process, both client-facing and team-facing
- plans the audit (time,scope,sampling,team,schedule,budget)
- conducts opening and interviews with auditee stakeholders
- reviews evidence, determines nonconformities, and finalizes the audit report

*Competency* --> Strong ISO 27001 knowledge and  understanding of accreditation requirements

* auditor
*Role* --> supports the lead auditor and performs audit activities
- conducts interviews with stakeholders (e.g, leaders, managers, controls owners)
- reviews documented evidence for ISO 27001 conformity
- drafts initial findings, identifies nonconformities, and tracks follow-up items

*Competency* --> knowledge of ISO 27001 and audit techniques, organizational and analytical skills

* technical expert or guide
*Role* --> provides specializeed knowledge in specific technical domains
- evaluates specific Annex A controls (e.g, cloud security, encryption, networking)
- guides the audit team on technical conformity issues
- may assist with language translation with non-nativee speakers

*Competence* --> subject matter or language-specific only

* observer
*Role* --> observes the audit for learning, oversight, or stakeholder representation
- gains experience or supervises the audit
- represents interested parties like regulators
- maintain confidentiality and impartiality
- does not opine on audit conclusions

*Competency* --> none required, but the lead auditor should brief the observer beforehand

** importance of audit team composition
- *Consistency* --> defined roles ensure audits achieve objectives across clients
- *Efficiency* --> clear roles enable effective collaboration and work distribution
- *Depth* --> a well-composed team with the right number of auditors and technical expertise ensures thorough audits
